YES / NO scenario matrix

Use this matrix to separate evidence that supports a direct-loss outcome from updates that point toward full mainnet-holder protection.

YES strengthens if…

  • Kelp or governance documents impose a haircut on all rsETH, including Ethereum mainnet.
  • Recovery funding fails and mainnet backing is diluted or impaired.
  • Withdrawals resume with less than full ETH/LST redemption value for direct holders.

NO strengthens if…

  • Recovery plan restores full backing before direct mainnet holders are haircut.
  • Losses are isolated to L2 bridged rsETH or bad-debt positions.
  • Mainnet rsETH redemptions are honored at the normal exchange ratio.

Ambiguous / volatility

  • Temporary withdrawal pauses are not necessarily direct loss.
  • Bad debt at Aave/Compound may matter only if passed through to direct rsETH holders.
  • Resolution wording and evidence standards will be critical.
